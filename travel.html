I got on my first aeroplane in 2012 at the age of 17, and it was to the USA; thus having the opportunity to visit a few states through a leadership scholarship in high school. Getting to have that experience, allowed me to dream and know that it's possible to see the world. And that has all that I've ever wanted.

Solo traveling became the medium, because I didn't want to wait until I was married or a millionaire in order to access the gift and opportunity of travel. I wanted to travel for myself, and gift myself that gift, I did.

Through my work as a consultant, I got the opportunity to learn about the impact of the arts industry, and I have been fascinated since. It's an expression that documents and shares the emotions of the time period, the subject and the intimacy of the artist through various mediums. I recently started collecting art, and it's the best decision ever! I only drink still or sparkling wines when it comes to alcohol beverages - simply because of the emotive tasting experience and the delicious, aromatic flavours.

The best country so far Kenya as the people are friendly and the startup ecosystem is thriving.
